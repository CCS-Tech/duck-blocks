Welcome to Duck Blocks!

The CMS for those who dislike Coding

Duck Blocks aims to add a bit of simplicity to the process of building a web site. We do this by giving people like you the ability to work with a grid in order to lay out and place content. You simply drag and drop, point and click, and build whatever you want! Our interface is aimed at making things dead simple and once your finished, your design will be converted into HTML, CSS, and JavaScript files that you can simply upload to your web hosting!
